Obtaining a Refferal

  1. Call (999) 999-9999
  2. Choose option 2
  3. In the voicemail leave: your name, your date of birth, a phone number where you can be reached (in case we have questions), the specialist/test/procedure that you are requesting, and the date of your appointment

We will have the refferal ready to pick up within 72 hours 

Pricing

Co-pays will be $15 to $25, depending on the service(s)

  • $30 for missed routine appointments
  • $50 for missed physical exams

You will not be charged if you cancel at least 24 hours prior to your appointment

Payment for services is expected at the time of service. We reserve the right to charge a $10 handling fee if your co-payment is not made at the time of service.
