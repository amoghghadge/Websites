Musikfest Chamber Orchestra

Taking auditions from now until November for advanced musicians and adults that want to participate in a chamber group. There is a winter and spring concert, and there are a couple rehearsals, including a dress rehearsal. Rehearsals are in a theater (also where concert happens) and are spread apart (socially distanced). Rehearsals are on Saturday morning from 10 to 12. Contact me for more information.
