Automated Call Center

Using Amazon Connect, I built an automated cloud call center for my mom's doctor office. People can call the office's phone number, and my call center will help route them to where they should go. When somebody calls, a prompt will play regarding which buttons to press. A patient can press 1 to schedule an appointment, 2 for refills on medication, 3 for referrals, etc. The call center is available at +1 539-476-2797. You can call the number to hear the prompt, but please do not actually press the buttons unless you want to make a call to my mom's doctor office. This is the GitHub repository.

OPM Alerts

OPM Alerts is a web application I made that independent offices can use to convey whether their offices are closed, delayed, or open. The website shows the Operating Status of Federal Offices in the Washington, DC area. This makes it easy for people with independent offices in the DC area to determine whether or not to close, delay, or open, as they can base their judgement off the operating status of the federal offices in DC. Then, they can send a text message to people regarding a judgement call on if their office will be closed, delayed, or open. I made this web app so that my mom, a doctor wither her own office, can use this website to message employees as well as patients whether or not the office is closed, delayed, or open. The web application is available at opm.amoghghadge.com, and this is the GitHub repository.
