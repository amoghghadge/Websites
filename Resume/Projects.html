AHWebdevs

AHWebdevs is a group that I founded in order to build websites for local businesses. I am the back-end developer, meaning I host the websites. I host them serverlessly through Amazon Web Services. I also design the websites themselves, for the local business to use. We do not charge anything to the businesses, because of the low cost that using a serverless architecture allows us to achieve. This is the GitHub repository.
